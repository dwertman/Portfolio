Goals

Define goals and purpose of site or organization in a paragraph or two. These questions will help get you started.

What is the name of your site?
My site is called DillSauceStudios.com, after the game studio I'd like to start
What is the purpose of this website?
The purpose of this website is to act as a rudimentary version of an actual website I would like for the game studio I plan to start.
What are the goals of your site?
This website will provide information on Dill Sauce Studios and some information about me. It will include completed school projects I've worked on. It will also serve as a place for people to share thoughts and experiences through blogs. This website will provide engaging content in a way that reflects my personality.
What do you intend to have this website do?
I intend for this website to hold some general information on the game studio I'd like to start, school projects I've completed, as well as provide a forum for discussions and blogs.
How will your site be different from other existing sites with similar purposes?
My site will be made up of mostly original content either generated by me through school projects and ideas for my game studio, or through others' stories.
Why will people come to your site? Who is your target audience?
For now, my target audience is the people that I can reach through word of mouth and social media about my website. But I hope to spark interest in other gamers and developers with my website.
